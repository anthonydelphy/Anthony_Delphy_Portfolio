Meteor Studio Website

Web Development for Public-facing Studio Website

Meteor Studio is an inter-disciplinary research laboratory at Arizona State University. Due to the large amount of products and research developed at Meteor Studio, the lab requires that it's website is frequently updated to accurately represent that activites of the studio. This task fall upon the Meteor Studio Design Team. This team is tasked with creating mock-ups within Figma for design iteration to developing these features on the website utilizing Bootstrap 4, JavaScript, and HTML5/CSS.

Webpage for the Coordinate Project

As the Design Team Manager at Meteor Studio through 2021-2023, it was my responsibility to oversee the development of the Meteor Studio website. This involved delegating website tasks to subordinates, regularly checking-in on their progress and assisting if necessary, as well as personally updating the website and developing features as needed.

Webpage for Meteor Talks/Presentations

This website developed by my team displays accurate information on the projects developed at Meteor Studio, up-to-date news articles/videos relating to the studio, and showcasing the teams who are/were employed at the laboratory. All pages on this website were developed to facilitate multiple devices and screen sizes.

Depending on the needs of the project manager/directors, new features would be developed as needed. New pages and features on the website would be designed, developed, and deployed typically within a few hours/days of request.
